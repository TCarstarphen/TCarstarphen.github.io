Welcome to your Zombie Survival Guide!

Zombies

The most important thing you need to know about zombies is that one shot to the head does it. Head shots are vital to your survival. And rememeber, if you get bit it's game over.

Tips

    Keep your clothes tight (to prevent snagging)
  • Duct tape it your friend! (Not only does it fix everything, but zombies can't bite through it)
  • Know your exits (Map out the starcases, the front and back entries, and even if there's roof access)
  • Have a partner! (You can't watch your own back)
  • Pack light (you gotta be ready to go at a moments notice and you don't want to waste time packing)
  • Do. Not. Get. Bit.
  • Most importantly: AIM FOR THE HEAD

Essentials
    • Canned food (lasts longer than perishables, duh)
    • Map of the area
    • Flashlight
    • Batteries
    • Hand crank radio
    • First aid kit
    • Water bottle
    • Matches
    • Knife (for hunting and gathering, not killing)
    • Cast iron skillet (multipurposeful, you can cook with it and use it to bash heads in)
Things to Avoid
  • Cities (Where there was once too many people, there will now be too many zombies)
  • The radio (You can listen, but don't respond. Assume everyone is the enemy)
  • Large groups (YOu can have a group of 3-4 people, but the larger the group, the louder you get)
  • Noise. It attracts the zombies.
